What This Calculator Actually Shows You

Risk (R)

The dollar amount between your entry and your stop. This is your actual risk per contract.

Reward

Target minus entry. Shows your real dollar upside, not wishful thinking.

R-Multiple

Reward ÷ Risk. If you don’t know this number, you are trading blindly.
