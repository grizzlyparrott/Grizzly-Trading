Palladium Microstructure: DOM, Sweep Risk, and Tape Reading

Palladium microstructure is the real reason the contract trades like a grenade. PA’s DOM is thin, fragmented, and unstable, which makes sweep risk enormous and tape signals violent. If you don’t understand how orders actually move through this book, you’re trading blind.

Why the PA DOM Looks Nothing Like GC or SI

Gold and Silver show stacked depth, layered liquidity, and consistent participation. Palladium shows the opposite — empty levels, vanishing bids, and size that evaporates when touched.

  • Depth is shallow across all price bands
  • Resting orders are tiny and unstable
  • Liquidity dries up instantly during volatility

This is the same structural fragility discussed in PA vs GC/SI liquidity.

Sweep Risk: The Hidden Killer in PA

Sweep risk is the probability that a single order clears multiple price levels because the book is too thin to absorb it. In PA, this isn’t rare — it’s normal.

  • Market orders sweep instantly through LVNs
  • Stop orders convert to market and cascade
  • Vertical moves form when both sides pull liquidity

These sweeps often trigger slippage spikes like the ones detailed in PA slippage behavior.

How PA Tape Reading Differs From Other Metals

Tape reading in PA isn’t about smooth flow — it’s about sudden changes in aggression. The tape reveals intent only when one side overwhelms the thin book.

Key tape signals in PA

  • Repeated small-lot hits → iceberg absorption
  • Sudden size pull → breakout or breakdown is imminent
  • One-sided sweep → squeezed shorts or panicked longs

The tape is cleaner in PA not because it’s stable — but because every real push leaves footprints.

How Liquidity Pockets Shape Microstructure

PA’s microstructure revolves around HVNs and LVNs. LVNs act like vacuum zones that cause instant jumps; HVNs act like brakes.

  • LVNs → sweep zones, fake stability
  • HVNs → consolidation zones with real absorption
  • Transition zones → slippage becomes unavoidable

These structures align directly with PA volume profile behavior.

You have to adjust execution to avoid becoming sweep fuel. PA punishes lazy entries harder than any metal.

PA Microstructure Will Punish Sloppy Execution

Thin DOM, sweepy tape, no forgiveness. Palladium’s book can’t absorb lazy entries. Read the tape, know where liquidity hides, and pick your spots — or get shredded fast.
