How 6A AUD/USD Price Action Changes Across Each Trading Session

6A AUD/USD price action is predictable once you understand how each global session behaves. Each one has different volatility, liquidity, and catalysts. If you trade all sessions the same way, you’re doing it wrong. Session behavior is one of the strongest edges in 6A.

Sydney Session: Slow Drip With Occasional Setup

Sydney is low liquidity and usually pointless for entries. Price often ranges tightly unless Australia releases economic data. Patterns you’ll see:

  • Small channels
  • Double tops/bottoms
  • Pre-Tokyo positioning

This session lines up with the pattern behavior covered in 6a-chart-patterns-that-work.html.

Tokyo Session: Real Movement Starts

Tokyo is critical for 6A because China’s data drops here. Expect:

  • Trend initiation
  • First breakout of the day
  • Strong reaction to China PMI, GDP, or Industrial Production

If there’s a macro driver (see swing-trading-6a-macro-themes.html), Tokyo is often where it starts showing on the chart.

London Session: Choppy Noise and Fakeouts

London is the trap session for 6A. Liquidity is higher, but most moves are fakeouts. London rarely creates the real trend—it sets up the trap for New York to resolve.

  • False breakouts
  • Whipsaw ranges
  • Liquidity grabs above/under obvious levels

Volume spikes here often tie into the fakeouts discussed in identifying-6a-reversals-volume-spikes.html.

New York Session: The Real Move of the Day

New York has the highest volatility and decides the daily trend. Expect:

  • Trend continuation after Tokyo
  • Breakouts with heavy volume
  • Big moves off U.S. data (CPI, NFP, Jobless Claims)

If you only trade one session for 6A, trade New York.

Table: How 6A Behaves in Each Session

Session Behavior Best Play
Sydney Low volatility, pre-positioning Range scalps
Tokyo Strong directional moves Breakout retests
London Chop and fakeouts Fade failed breaks
New York Real trend development Ride continuation setups

Bottom line

6A’s price action changes drastically by session. Sydney builds the range, Tokyo sets direction, London fakes, and New York decides the truth. Learn each session’s personality and 6A becomes a predictable market instead of a random chart.
