How to Use DOM and Time & Sales on ES

Most beginners stare at the DOM and Time & Sales like it’s encrypted data. ES order flow isn’t mystical — it’s just showing who is hitting the bids, who is lifting the offers, and where liquidity is stacking or vanishing. If you know how to read it, you stop entering blindly and start timing trades with actual intent behind them.

What the DOM Actually Tells You

The DOM (Depth of Market) displays:

  • Bids and offers at each price level
  • How much size is sitting there
  • How quickly that size appears or disappears

The DOM shows where liquidity wants to trade — or wants to pretend it wants to trade. On ES, spoofing and fake size are common, so focus on reaction, not static levels.

DOM SignalMeaning
Bids stacking and holdingSupport forming
Offers stacking and holdingResistance forming
Size disappearing before trades hitFake liquidity
Fast bid/ask depletionMomentum shift

What Time & Sales Shows

Time & Sales is the tape — actual executed orders. It tells you:

  • Whether traders are lifting the offer (aggressive buying)
  • Whether they’re hitting the bid (aggressive selling)
  • Speed and aggression of trades
  • Large prints signaling institutions

How to Use DOM + Time & Sales Together

1. Confirm Breakouts

If ES breaks a key level, you want:

  • Offers getting lifted aggressively
  • Bid support building under the breakout level
  • Fast prints hitting the tape
If none of this shows up, the breakout is weak and likely to fail.

2. Confirm Reversals

A real reversal shows:

  • Bid absorption slowing the drop
  • Large buyers hitting offers at the turn
  • Stacked offers getting pulled (liquidity vacuum)

3. Sniff Out Fake Liquidity

If 500 lots are sitting on the bid but disappear seconds before price reaches them, that size was never real. Don’t base trades on static DOM — watch liquidity behavior.

4. Enhance Your Level Tests

At key levels like VWAP, session high/low, or the opening range, the DOM slows down before the turn. The tape flips direction. This is how pros confirm entries.

If you don't know how to use these levels yet, read Session Highs/Lows & VWAP.

Order Flow Is the Foundation, Not a Flex

DOM and Time & Sales aren’t advanced — they’re table stakes. Read aggression, spot liquidity shifts, and track how levels react. Entries tighten, stops shrink, and fakeouts stop catching you off guard.
