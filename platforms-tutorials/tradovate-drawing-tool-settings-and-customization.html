Tradovate Drawing Tool Settings & Customization Guide

Tradovate’s drawing tools work fine out of the box, but their default settings are mid. If your lines are too thick, your colors too loud, or your fibs unreadable, you’re making your charts harder than they need to be. This guide shows how to clean up every drawing tool so your charts stay sharp and fast.

Accessing the Drawing Tool Settings

Right-click any drawing → Settings. Or open the drawing toolbar → click the gear icon for global defaults.

These two menus control different things:

MenuPurpose
Object SettingsChanges one drawing
Global SettingsChanges defaults for all future drawings

Trendline Customization

Trendlines are the backbone of structure. Make them clean:

  • Color: White or bright yellow
  • Thickness: 1 px
  • Extend left/right: Off (unless needed)
  • Show price labels: Optional

If your trendlines look like ropes, you’re doing it wrong.

Zone / Rectangle Customization

Zones should be obvious but not obnoxious:

  • Fill Color: Light blue or gray
  • Opacity: 20–35%
  • Border: Off or minimal
  • Extend Right: On for supply/demand

Too-bright zones ruin chart readability.

Fibonacci Retracement Custom Settings

Tradovate’s default fib palette is ugly and confusing. Fix it:

  • Enable only key levels (38.2, 50, 61.8)
  • Use simple colors: one color for levels, one for labels
  • Set line thickness to 1
  • Disable “reverse direction” unless needed

This keeps fibs useful instead of clutter.

Anchor Behavior & Snapping

Under global drawing settings:

  • Snap to OHLC: ON (prevents sloppy anchor placement)
  • Snap to Grid: OFF
  • Show anchor points: Small

Snapping to candle highs/lows makes drawings precise without effort.

Saving Custom Defaults

After customizing a tool, click Set as Default. This saves the style for every future drawing of that type.

Once you set defaults, drawing becomes muscle memory instead of busywork.

Final Thoughts

Clean drawings make clean decisions. Tradovate gives you full control—use it. Take five minutes to customize your drawing tools and your charts instantly become easier to read and trade.
