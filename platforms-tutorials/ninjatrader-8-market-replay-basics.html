NinjaTrader 8 Market Replay Basics

Market Replay in NinjaTrader 8 lets you practice trading using real historical tick data. It behaves like a live market—orders fill, price moves tick by tick, and your ATM strategies run normally. This is the safest way to build skill without burning cash or blowing a prop evaluation.

What You Can Do in Market Replay

  • Practice entries and exits
  • Run ATM strategies
  • Test chart setups
  • Replay key sessions (CPI, FOMC, open, etc.)
  • Experience fast volatility without real risk

It’s the closest thing to live trading without actually trading live.

Step 1: Download Replay Data

From the Control Center:

  1. Go to Tools → Historical Data
  2. Click the Load Replay Data tab
  3. Select your instrument (ES, MNQ, CL, etc.)
  4. Pick the date(s) you want
  5. Click Download

You can download days, weeks, or months. Bigger files = longer download, but more practice material.

Step 2: Switch to Playback Connection

Disconnect from any live or SIM feed.

  1. Go to Connections
  2. Click Playback Connection

You’ll see a playback control window appear.

Step 3: Load Your Instrument and Chart

Open a chart for the same instrument you downloaded replay data for.

  • If you downloaded ES replay → open an ES chart
  • If the chart says “No data available,” you picked a date you don’t have data for

Step 4: Using Playback Controls

The playback window includes:

ControlPurpose
Play/PauseStart or stop replay
Speed SliderReplay 1x → 500x speed
Date SelectorChoose specific replay day/time
Jump ButtonsSkip forward/backward

Replay speed is the real power. Want to watch price action at 20x? Click it. Want to slow down a fast reversal? Drop it to 1x.

Step 5: Practice Trading

Now just trade like normal:

  • Place orders with Chart Trader
  • Use the DOM for scalping practice
  • Watch your ATM brackets fire
  • Manage positions in real time

Everything behaves exactly as it would live. That’s why this tool is so valuable.

Key Notes About Replay Data

  • Replay uses tick-by-tick recorded data
  • If your chart isn’t moving, you’re probably outside recorded hours
  • You can replay both RTH and ETH
  • Replay is local — storing months of it takes space

Performance Tips

  • Don’t load dozens of charts at once
  • Limit indicator usage
  • Replay one instrument per workspace if possible
  • SSD drives load replay data faster

The faster your storage, the smoother replay feels.

Final Thoughts

NinjaTrader 8 Market Replay is one of the most powerful training tools available. You can practice real market conditions, test strategies, and build confidence without risking a dollar. If you're serious about leveling up, replay should be part of your routine.
