Bookmap Add-Ons Overview: Core Tools That Enhance Order Flow

Bookmap comes with several official add-ons that expose deeper layers of order flow. Some of these tools highlight hidden liquidity, others track aggressive players, and some reveal momentum shifts that the heatmap alone can’t catch. This overview shows what each add-on does so you know which ones matter and which you can ignore.

Why Add-Ons Matter

The base Bookmap view shows:

  • heatmap (resting liquidity)
  • volume bubbles/dots (executed trades)
  • DOM columns

Add-ons expose the extra stuff traders care about:

  • hidden orders
  • absorption battles
  • stop runs and sweeps
  • large lot bursts
  • pressure changes

We already covered some individually, but this page ties them together and covers the rest.

1. Iceberg Detection

Detects hidden limit orders that refill after execution.

Shows when a big player is:

  • absorbing aggressive flow
  • defending a level
  • building a position quietly

We went into detail earlier: Iceberg Detection (if already created).

2. Absorption Indicator

Shows when one side is absorbing aggressive order flow without letting price move.

  • stops fake breakouts
  • reveals hidden intentions
  • often appears ahead of reversals

Works even better when paired with CVD.

3. Sweep / Stop-Run Indicator

Detects when multiple levels get cleared fast by aggressive orders.

  • confirms real momentum
  • highlights stop cascades
  • shows where levels break for real

Deep dive is here: Sweeps and Stop Runs.

4. Strength Meter

Shows the relative aggression of buyers and sellers across time.

Useful for:

  • spotting trend acceleration
  • seeing power imbalances
  • filtering fake moves

Strong meter spikes often align with bubble streaks and CVD surges.

5. Large Lot Tracker

Highlights large aggressive transactions hitting the tape.

  • tracks big traders
  • shows where they enter or exit
  • helps identify the true driver of a move

If bubbles are the “what,” large lots are the “who.”

6. Liquidity Tracker Tools

Tracks:

  • resting liquidity strength
  • liquidity movement (chasing/pulling)
  • major resting clusters

Great for understanding who’s defending a level and where the market wants to go.

7. DOM Pro Tools

Enhances the depth-of-market with:

  • better imbalance views
  • cumulative depth readings
  • highlighting book structure changes

Traders who live on the DOM will appreciate this one.

How to Enable Add-Ons

  1. Go to Settings → Add-Ons
  2. Choose the add-on you want
  3. Enable it and configure the sensitivity/thresholds
  4. Save your layout when you’re done

Which Add-Ons Are Actually Worth Using?

If you want the most important ones:

  • Icebergs
  • Absorption
  • Sweeps/Stop Runs
  • Large Lot Tracker

These four cover 90% of the informational edge Bookmap can give you.

Bookmap Add-Ons Turn Noise Into Signal

The right Bookmap add-ons reveal what raw price can’t — hidden orders, absorption, sweeps, and large player behavior. You don’t need them all. Just choose what fits your style, tune the thresholds, and lock in a workspace that filters noise. The more precise your setup, the cleaner your read on order flow.
