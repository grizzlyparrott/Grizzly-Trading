Privacy Policy

Grizzly Parrot Trading respects your privacy. This page explains what information is collected, how it’s used, and what you can expect when visiting this website.

Information We Collect

This site does not require accounts, logins, or personal submissions. The only data collected is standard, non-personal technical information:

  • Basic analytics (page views, traffic patterns)
  • Browser type and device information
  • Approximate location (country-level)
  • Referring sites

This data cannot identify you personally. It’s used only to understand how readers use the site so the content can be improved.

Cookies

Grizzly Parrot Trading uses minimal cookies through Google Analytics to measure site performance. These cookies do not store personal information and are standard across most websites.

Third-Party Services

The site may use common tools like:

  • Google Analytics (traffic measurement)
  • Embedded charts or widgets from trusted providers

These services have their own independent privacy policies. This site does not sell, trade, or share your data with any third party.

No Financial or Sensitive Data Collected

This site does not collect:

  • Names
  • Emails
  • Payment information
  • Addresses
  • Trading records
  • Any personally identifiable information

There are no forms, memberships, or submissions on this site.

External Links

Some articles may link to outside websites. Once you leave this site, the external website’s privacy policy applies, not ours.

Policy Updates

If the privacy policy ever changes, this page will be updated. Content visitors will not be contacted individually.
