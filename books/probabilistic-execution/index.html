What you will actually get

No theory dump. No "just control your emotions" pep talks. You get concrete rules and structures you can bolt directly onto your trading.

  • Risk and sizing framework. How to define account risk, per-trade risk, and daily pain limits that you can actually stick to.
  • Volatility-based sizing. Why the same contract size is not the same risk every day, and how to normalize it.
  • Scaling rules. When to add, when to cut, and when to flatten so you are not winging it mid-trade.
  • Execution playbooks. Step-by-step flows from "idea" to "flattened" so you stop improvising with real money.
  • Risk limits with teeth. Daily and weekly rules that shut you down before you spiral, not after.
  • Post-trade review templates. Simple ways to track behavior and tighten up without drowning in spreadsheets.
You will not find magic indicators, secret entries, or "100% win rate" garbage. This book assumes the edge you need is fixing how you execute, not adding more signals.
